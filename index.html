◆ 2:30 PM — Pick your afternoon
Recommended
Elephanta Caves
Ferry from Gateway. ~1 hr each way, 1 hr at the caves. UNESCO site. 6th century rock-cut temples. Last ferry back ~5:30 PM.
Slower Afternoon
Cafe Veronica's
Stay in Colaba. Browse the market, sit here for cold coffee. A very specific old-Bombay atmosphere.
6:00
PM
Haji Ali Dargah Sight
Check tides before going — causeway floods at high tide. Island mosque, sea on both sides. The setting does all the work.
⏱ 45 min
7:30
PM
Bandra-Worli Sea Link In transit
Ask your cab to take the Sealink back to Bandra. Worth it once at dusk.

Dinner

Light + Coffee
Subko Cacao

Nandan Coffee for something light; Mercii for afternoon drinks and food. Both in Bandra.

PM
Siddhivinayak Temple Temple
Prabhadevi, 20 min from Bandra. Ganesh deity, ornate interiors, serious devotional energy. Weekday afternoons have shorter queues.

5:00
PM

Pedder Road. A proper curated museum — not a theme park. Genuinely underrated. If you have any interest in Bollywood history, this is the right version of it.
